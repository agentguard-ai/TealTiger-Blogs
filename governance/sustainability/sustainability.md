# Sustainability and Responsible AI Operations

Sustainability in AI governance is not only environmental — it is **operational, financial, and organizational**.

TealTiger treats sustainability as a governance outcome.

## Dimensions of AI Sustainability

### Operational Sustainability
- Predictable behavior
- Controlled autonomy
- Stable system boundaries

### Financial Sustainability
- Governed model selection
- Cost-aware execution policies
- Prevention of runaway agent behavior

### Organizational Sustainability
- Clear accountability
- Reduced governance fatigue
- Repeatable assurance processes

## Governance as the Enabler

By enforcing deterministic governance:
- Systems remain controllable as they scale
- Costs remain predictable
- Risk does not compound silently

## Outcome

Sustainable AI systems are **governed systems** — not unmanaged autonomous ones.
