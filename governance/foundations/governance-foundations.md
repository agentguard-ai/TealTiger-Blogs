# Governance Foundations for Agentic AI

TealTiger governance starts with a **contract-first, deterministic foundation**. Governance is not an afterthought or an advisory layer — it is a system property.

## Why Foundations Matter

Without foundations, governance becomes probabilistic, inconsistent, and impossible to audit. TealTiger establishes **explicit contracts** that define what agents are allowed to do *before* execution.

## Core Principles

### Determinism by Design
Every governance decision produces the same outcome for the same inputs.

### Contract-First Enforcement
Governance rules are declared, versioned, and frozen before deployment.

### Policy as Infrastructure
Governance policies are deployed alongside code, not embedded in prompts.

## What TealTiger Enforces
- Allowed agent roles
- Approved tools and capabilities
- Data domains and purposes
- Risk boundaries

## Outcome

Governance becomes **repeatable, testable, and auditable** — suitable for enterprise adoption.
