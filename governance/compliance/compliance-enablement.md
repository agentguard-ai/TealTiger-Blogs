# Compliance Enablement with TealTiger

TealTiger is **compliance-enabling, not compliance-completing**.

## What TealTiger Provides

- Deterministic controls
- Immutable evidence
- Policy-to-control traceability

## Supported Frameworks

- NIST AI RMF
- ISO/IEC 42001
- EU AI Act (control evidence)

## Important Disclaimer

Final compliance accountability always remains with the organization.

## Outcome

TealTiger reduces compliance friction by making governance verifiable and repeatable.
