Compliance Enablement explains how TealTiger helps organizations **support regulatory and standards‑based requirements** without claiming to replace formal compliance programs.

TealTiger is **compliance‑enabling, not compliance‑completing**.  
It provides deterministic controls, verifiable enforcement, and auditable evidence that organizations can use as part of their broader compliance posture.

## What This Hub Covers

- Translating regulatory requirements into enforceable controls
- Producing technical evidence for audits and assessments
- Mapping governance enforcement to compliance frameworks
- Supporting continuous compliance readiness

## Compliance Context

TealTiger is designed to support evidence generation and control enforcement for frameworks such as:

- NIST AI Risk Management Framework (AI RMF)
- ISO/IEC 42001
- EU AI Act (technical and operational control evidence)

Final compliance responsibility always remains with the organization.
