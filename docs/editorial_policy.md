Editorial Policy — blogs.tealtiger.ai
_Last updated: 2026-03-03
Purpose
blogs.tealtiger.ai is a professional AI Governance publication. Posts must help readers make decisions and operate controls across the AI lifecycle.
Principles
People-first: write for practitioners; avoid hype.
Evidence-first: cite sources; distinguish fact vs opinion.
Scope clarity: state what applies to TealTiger v1.1 vs future work.
Actionability: include "What to do next" in every post.
Non-claims: no compliance certification claims.
Quality checklist
Clear problem statement and why it matters
Practical guidance (controls, templates, checklists)
Links to primary sources where possible
TealTiger Lens section where relevant
